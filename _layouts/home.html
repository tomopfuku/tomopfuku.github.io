About


Welcome to the Parins-Fukuchi lab at University of Toronto! The lab tackles broad evolutionary questions by developing new computational approaches. How do complex, multivariate phenotypes evolve? What processes drive the emergence of novelty from the phenotypic to the genomic levels? How can we best harness emerging data sources to robustly infer evolutionary patterns and processes across the tree of life? A central theme involves merging insights from the fossil record with those from genomic data, using organisms ranging from hominins and other Primates to plants.
